The Evolution

It was SEO.
Then GEO.
Soon ASO.
It's all IRO.

The platforms keep changing. The tools keep changing. The acronyms keep changing. But the fundamental truth never does: users signal intent before they decide, and your job is to be the answer when that intent fires. We identify where your audience's intent lives, find the gaps no tool can surface, and build programs that put you in front of the right people at the exact moment they're ready to act.

Past

SEO — Search Engine Optimization

Rank on Google. Get clicks. Drive traffic. Keywords, backlinks, and technical hygiene. It worked — and still does. But it's table stakes now.

Now

GEO — Generative Engine Optimization

Show up in ChatGPT answers. Appear in Google AI Overviews. Get cited by Perplexity. Search has left the blue links.

Soon

ASO — Agent Search Optimization

AI agents will browse, compare, and buy on behalf of users. When an agent searches for the best solution — will it find you?

Always

Senior professionals only. No account managers relaying messages, no juniors learning on your budget. The people in the strategy call are the people doing the work — every engagement is led by practitioners with 10+ years of experience.

+

We've delivered results across B2B SaaS, eCommerce, publishing, fintech, beauty, and consumer tech. But we don't rely on industry templates — we research your specific market, intent landscape, and competitive dynamics from scratch.

Does your consultancy cover implementation?

+

Yes. We don't hand off decks and disappear. Cut Inside handles strategy, execution, and ongoing optimization. If you have an in-house team, we integrate with them. If you don't, we operate as your search team.

What makes Cut Inside different from other SEO agencies?

+

Three things: we only staff seniors, we treat AI search as a first-class channel (not an afterthought), and we measure success by business outcomes — not keyword counts. Our IRO framework captures intent across every platform where your buyers are searching.

What is IRO, and how is it different from SEO?

+

IRO — Intent Response Optimization — is our framework for capturing demand wherever it fires: Google, ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, AI Overviews, and whatever comes next. SEO is one channel within IRO. The platform is irrelevant; the intent is what matters.
